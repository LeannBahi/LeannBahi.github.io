MAGPIE

Rapid Prototyping

  

BACKGROUND

The Children’s School at CMU challenged the Rapid Design and Prototyping for Computer systems class to help track the locations and activities of all members of the school and help make dismissal an easier and less stressful process.

MY ROLE

For phase 1, I was the leader for the Human Computer Interaction team. For phases 2 and 3 I was the interface and interaction designer.

CHALLENGES

How can we better track student locations, interests, and activities?
How can we make dismissal less stressful and more efficient?
How can we do all of this while staying out of the way?
