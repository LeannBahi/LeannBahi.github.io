TRUE SOUND

Visual Arts

BACKGROUND

These six pages were created by hand tracing the sound waves created by different voicemail recordings. These were then soaked in water and left to dry. The six soundwaves were then scanned and hand bound into one book. This project plays with the idea of the unsaid behind words.
